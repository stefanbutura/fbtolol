Credits to https://github.com/mathzx/zxlolbot for League of Legends bot functionalities.

A simple script to connect your Facebook and League of Legends account.

You can chat directly from the League of Legends client by sending messages to a bot account
which sends them to a Facebook user.

Requires:

- sleekxmpp
- dnspython
- fbchat

Edit fbtolol.py with your data
